📦 What is GoDrop?

GoDrop is a WhatsApp-first logistics operating system built for African cities. Many deliveries still rely on fragmented chats, manual coordination, unclear addresses, and inconsistent rider availability, leading to delays and inefficient operations.

GoDrop structures this process by organizing customer requests, preparing dispatch-ready delivery jobs, and coordinating rider assignment through familiar WhatsApp workflows. Already operating in live delivery environments, GoDrop is building the logistics coordination infrastructure designed for the realities of emerging markets.

Yes. We handle park pickups, waybills, and other special delivery requests. Additional handling fees may apply depending on the location.
How do I request a delivery? +
Simply send your pickup and drop-off details through WhatsApp and GoDrop will coordinate the process.
